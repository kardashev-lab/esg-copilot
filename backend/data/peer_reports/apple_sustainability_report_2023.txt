APPLE SUSTAINABILITY REPORT 2023

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Apple's 2023 sustainability report showcases our commitment to environmental responsibility, social impact, and ethical business practices. This report covers our fiscal year 2023 (October 1, 2022 - September 30, 2023) and demonstrates progress toward our 2030 carbon neutrality goal.

ENVIRONMENTAL PERFORMANCE

Climate Action
- Scope 1 emissions: 15,200 tCO2e (reduced 12% from FY2022)
- Scope 2 emissions: 85,000 tCO2e (reduced 8% from FY2022)
- Scope 3 emissions: 12,500,000 tCO2e (reduced 5% from FY2022)
- Total emissions: 12,600,200 tCO2e
- Renewable energy: 85% of total energy consumption
- Target: 100% renewable energy by 2025

Carbon Neutrality Progress
- 100% of corporate operations carbon neutral since 2020
- 50% reduction in product carbon footprint since 2015
- 95% of suppliers committed to 100% renewable energy
- Target: 100% carbon neutral across entire value chain by 2030

Sustainable Materials
- 20% recycled materials in all products
- 100% recycled aluminum in iPhone 15 enclosures
- 100% recycled rare earth elements in iPhone 15
- 100% recycled cobalt in iPhone 15 batteries
- Target: 100% recycled materials in all products by 2030

Waste Reduction
- 100% of manufacturing waste diverted from landfill
- 95% of packaging materials are recyclable
- 50,000+ tons of e-waste recycled through Apple Trade In
- Target: Zero waste to landfill by 2025

Water Stewardship
- 25% reduction in water use per unit in manufacturing
- 100% of Tier 1 suppliers have water management programs
- 20% reduction in water use in data centers
- Target: 30% reduction in water use per unit by 2025

SOCIAL PERFORMANCE

Labor Rights
- 100% of Tier 1 suppliers audited for labor standards
- 98% of suppliers meet Apple's labor standards
- 8 supplier code of conduct violations identified and resolved
- 100,000+ workers trained on labor rights and safety
- Target: 100% of suppliers to meet labor standards by 2025

Diversity and Inclusion
- 45% of global workforce are women
- 40% of leadership positions held by women
- 50% of new hires are from underrepresented groups
- 20% of senior leadership from underrepresented groups
- Target: 50% of leadership positions held by women by 2025

Worker Safety
- 0.5 lost-time injury rate per 100 workers
- 50 hours of safety training per worker annually
- 100% of workers covered by health and safety programs
- Target: Zero workplace fatalities

Community Impact
- $200 million invested in community programs
- 5 million students reached through education programs
- 1 million+ volunteer hours by Apple employees
- 100+ community partnerships established

GOVERNANCE PERFORMANCE

Board Diversity
- 7 out of 12 board members are women (58%)
- 5 out of 12 board members are from underrepresented groups (42%)
- 10 out of 12 board members are independent (83%)
- Average board tenure: 6 years

Executive Compensation
- 20% of executive compensation linked to ESG performance
- ESG metrics include: emissions reduction, supplier compliance, diversity targets
- Annual ESG performance review for all executives

Risk Management
- Climate risk assessment completed
- Supply chain risk assessment completed
- Human rights risk assessment completed
- ESG risks integrated into enterprise risk management

Stakeholder Engagement
- 1,000+ stakeholder meetings conducted
- 25,000+ stakeholder feedback responses received
- 50 stakeholder advisory panels established
- Regular engagement with investors, NGOs, and communities

CONCLUSION

Apple's 2023 sustainability report demonstrates significant progress toward our sustainability goals while acknowledging the challenges that remain. We are committed to accelerating our efforts to create a more sustainable future for all stakeholders.

Key achievements in FY2023:
- Reduced carbon emissions by 6%
- Increased renewable energy to 85%
- Improved supplier compliance to 98%
- Enhanced board diversity to 58% women
- Invested $1 billion in sustainability

Challenges and opportunities:
- Accelerating carbon neutrality across value chain
- Expanding renewable energy adoption
- Strengthening supply chain resilience
- Enhancing stakeholder engagement
- Scaling sustainable innovation

Apple remains committed to our purpose of making technology accessible to everyone, and we will continue to lead the industry in sustainable business practices.
